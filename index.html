Yellowstone Trip Planner - 3-6 October 2024

Join WhatsApp Group

Trip Overview

Day 1 (Thursday, October 3rd)

5:00 AM: Depart Boulder, CO

1:00 PM: Arrive at Grand Teton National Park (8 hours drive)

  • Quick stop at Jackson Lake Overlook (15 minutes)
  • Optional: Short walk at Jenny Lake (30 minutes)

2:00 PM: Depart Grand Teton

3:00 PM: Enter Yellowstone through South Entrance (1 hour drive)

3:30 PM: Stop at West Thumb Geyser Basin (30 minutes drive)

  • 45-minute walk around the boardwalk
  • See Lake Shore Group, Big Cone, Thumb Paint Pots

5:00 PM: Depart for Madison Campground (1 hour drive)

6:00 PM: Arrive at Madison Campground, set up camp

6:30 PM: Dinner at campsite

7:00 PM: Evening wildlife drive in Madison area (1 hour)

  • Look for elk and bison in the meadows

8:00 PM: Return to camp, relax or stargazing

Alternative: If arriving later, skip West Thumb and go directly to Madison Campground.

Day 2 (Friday, October 4th)

6:00 AM: Breakfast at Madison camp

7:00 AM: Drive to Old Faithful area (30 minutes)

7:30 AM: Explore Upper Geyser Basin (2.5 hours)

  • See Old Faithful eruption
  • Walk the boardwalks to see other geysers (Castle, Riverside, Grand)

10:00 AM: Drive to Midway Geyser Basin (15 minutes)

10:15 AM: See Grand Prismatic Spring and Excelsior Geyser (1 hour)

11:30 AM: Drive to Lower Geyser Basin (10 minutes)

11:40 AM: Visit Fountain Paint Pot area (50 minutes)

12:30 PM: Picnic lunch at Firehole Lake Drive picnic area (30 minutes)

1:30 PM: Drive Firehole Lake Drive (30 minutes)

  • See Great Fountain Geyser, White Dome Geyser

2:00 PM: Drive to Norris Geyser Basin (30 minutes)

2:30 PM: Explore Norris Geyser Basin (1 hour)

3:30 PM: Drive to Mammoth Hot Springs (45 minutes)

4:15 PM: Arrive at Mammoth Campground, set up camp (45 minutes)

5:00 PM: Explore Mammoth Hot Springs Terraces (2 hours)

  • Upper Terrace Drive
  • Walk boardwalks at Lower Terraces

7:00 PM: Dinner at Mammoth Hot Springs Dining Room or at camp

8:30 PM: Evening walk around Mammoth Hot Springs Historic District or stargazing

Alternative: If running behind schedule, skip Norris Geyser Basin and go directly to Mammoth.

Day 3 (Saturday, October 5th)

5:00 AM: Early wake up, breakfast at camp

5:30 AM: Break down camp and pack up

6:15 AM: Depart Mammoth Campground

7:15 AM: Arrive in Lamar Valley (1 hour drive)

7:15 AM: Morning wildlife viewing in Lamar Valley (2.5 hours)

  • Look for wolves, bears, bison, elk

9:45 AM: Drive to Tower Fall (30 minutes)

10:15 AM: View Tower Fall (30 minutes)

10:45 AM: Drive to Canyon area (45 minutes)

11:30 AM: Lunch at Canyon Village (45 minutes)

12:15 PM: Grand Canyon of Yellowstone (2.5 hours)

  • View Lower Falls from Artist Point
  • North Rim Drive, stop at Inspiration Point

2:45 PM: Drive to Lake Yellowstone Hotel (1 hour)

3:45 PM: Arrive at Lake Yellowstone Hotel, check-in

4:15 PM: Free time to relax, explore lake area, or refresh

6:00 PM: Dinner at Lake Yellowstone Hotel restaurant

Alternatives:

  • If you prefer more time for wildlife viewing, you could extend your time in Lamar Valley and shorten your visit to the Grand Canyon of Yellowstone.
  • If you're particularly interested in geology, you could add a stop at the Mud Volcano area (between Canyon and Lake Hotel) instead of free time at the hotel.
Day 4 (Sunday, October 6th)

5:30 AM: Early breakfast at hotel

6:15 AM: Sunrise at Yellowstone Lake (30 minutes)

7:00 AM: Short walk on Pelican Creek Nature Trail (45 minutes)

8:00 AM: Visit Mud Volcano area (1 hour)

9:30 AM: Final drive through Hayden Valley for wildlife (1 hour)

11:00 AM: Return to Lake Village for lunch (30 minutes)

12:00 PM: Begin drive back to Boulder via East Entrance

8:00 PM: Estimated arrival in Boulder (8 hours drive)

Alternative: If preferring a more leisurely morning, skip Pelican Creek Trail and spend more time at Mud Volcano or Hayden Valley.
